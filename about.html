About Fast Free Convert

Founded in 2023, Fast Free Convert was created to solve the frustrations people face when working with PDF & All documents every day.

Fast Free Convert began as a simple idea between two friends who were frustrated with existing File Convert solutions. Sumit, a developer, and Payal, a designer, saw how people struggled with expensive, complicated File Convert software and decided to build something better.

What started as a side project quickly grew into a full-fledged platform serving thousands of users worldwide. Our mission is to make Fast Free Converter accessible, affordable, and easy to use for everyone - from students to business professionals.

Today, Easy Convert offers a complete suite of Convert solutions, all available right in your browser with no installations required. We're proud to have helped over 500,000 users work with their documents more efficiently.
